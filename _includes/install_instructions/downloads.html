Download files

You need to download some files to follow the lessons in this workshop.

  1. Download shell-lesson-data.zip and move the file to your Desktop.
  2. Unzip/extract the files. Let your instructor know if you need help with this step. You should end up with a new folder called shell-lesson-data on your Desktop.
  3. Download python-novice-gapminder-data.zip and unzip/extract the files somewhere memorable (such as in a python-lesson-data folder on your Desktop). Keep the data files in the data folder.
